# KPI examples for finance

Money:

*  Sales growth

*  Improved cash flow

*  ROI or ROE

Customer value:

*  Customer on-time delivery %

*  Customer lead time reductions

*  Customer satisfaction ratings

Operations improvements:

*  Inventory turns

*  Overall trhoughput

*  Lead time reductions

Productivity improvements:

*  Overall efficiency % gains

*  Sales per employee
